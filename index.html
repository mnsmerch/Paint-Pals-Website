The cost to paint a house varies based on size, condition, and the scope of work. For a typical 2,000-2,500 sq ft home in the Highlands Ranch area, exterior painting usually ranges from $4,500 to $8,500, while interior painting ranges from $3,500 to $7,000. We provide free, detailed estimates so you know exactly what to expect — no hidden fees or surprises.

With Colorado's intense sun, temperature swings, and occasional hail, exterior paint typically lasts 5-10 years depending on the quality of paint and prep work. At Paint Pals, we use premium paints from Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore, combined with thorough surface preparation, to ensure your paint job lasts as long as possible. We also offer guidance on maintenance to extend the life of your finish.

Most interior projects take 2-4 days, while exterior projects typically take 3-5 days, depending on the size and condition of your home.
